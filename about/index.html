About Doug Bullis

I’ve worked closely with brokers across Washington, including serving as an instructor with NWMLS. That experience provided direct insight into how brokers actually operate day-to-day — what works, where things break down, and where most people get stuck.

What I Focus On

  • Broker growth and business development

How I Think

I approach real estate through a systems and process lens. With a Lean Six Sigma background, I focus on simplifying workflows, eliminating inefficiencies, and building repeatable systems that support long-term growth.

The goal isn’t complexity — it’s clarity, consistency, and better outcomes over time.
