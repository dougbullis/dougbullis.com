About Doug Bullis

I’m a Managing Broker in Washington State with a background in brokerage operations, agent development, and real estate systems.

Over the years, I’ve worked closely with brokers across Washington, including serving as an instructor with NWMLS. That experience gave me a front-row view into how agents actually operate day-to-day — what works, what breaks down, and where most people get stuck.

What I Focus On

My work centers on helping real estate professionals operate more efficiently and with greater clarity. That includes:

  • Brokerage operations and structure
  • Agent growth and business development
  • Transaction flow and process improvement
  • Systems that reduce friction and increase consistency

How I Think

I approach real estate through a process and systems lens. With a Lean Six Sigma background, I focus on simplifying workflows, removing inefficiencies, and building repeatable systems that support long-term growth.

The goal isn’t complexity — it’s clarity, consistency, and better outcomes over time.
