About Doug Bullis

I provide consulting support to real estate brokerages focused on operations, compliance, and workflow design.

My approach is grounded in process clarity, system consistency, and practical execution — not theory. The objective is to help brokerages operate more effectively, reduce friction, and build structures that hold up under real-world conditions.

Drawing from experience as a former Managing Broker and NWMLS instructor, I bring a working knowledge of how brokerages function at a day-to-day level, including the operational gaps that impact performance, risk, and scalability.

Core Focus

  • Brokerage operations and workflow structure
  • Regulatory compliance and risk awareness
  • Process standardization and consistency
  • Training, system adoption, and execution
  • Operational systems that support scalability

Approach

Most operational issues in real estate are not caused by lack of effort — they stem from inconsistent processes, unclear systems, and gaps in execution. My work focuses on identifying those breakdowns and replacing them with structured, repeatable workflows.

With a Lean Six Sigma background, I emphasize simplification, clarity, and measurable improvement rather than unnecessary complexity.

Experience Context

Over the course of my career, I’ve worked closely with brokers across Washington State, delivering training, supporting system adoption, and helping improve day-to-day operational performance. That experience provides a clear perspective on what works in practice — and what doesn’t.
